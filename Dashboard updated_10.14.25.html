Guide Statewide Math Professional Learning in California

MPLP Survey Dashboard - Data collected through: October 14, 2025.

This survey guides the Mathematics Professional Learning Partnership Grant (MPLP) work. Kern County Superintendent of Schools (KCSOS) was awarded the MPLP grant in April 2025 to develop statewide professional learning on the 2023 Mathematics Framework for coaches and teacher leaders. KCSOS is partnering with WestEd, a nonpartisan education research agency, on this survey. The dashboard provides summarized responses on the main items of the survey.

Interest in NCTM Effective Mathematics Teaching Practices

The 2023 Mathematics Framework integrates eight National Council of Teachers of Mathematics (NCTM) effective mathematics teaching practices. Participants ranked these practices based on their interest in learning more (1 = most curious, 8 = least curious). Lower average rankings indicate higher interest.
